about me

Marina M : Data Analyst, Designer and Storyteller. My goal is to bring Art and Design to the research of Data Analysis.

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a passionate and driven individual with a diverse background that encompasses graphic design, multilingual communication, and data analytics. Allow me to share a glimpse of my professional journey thus far. During my dynamic Graphic Design Internship at O'Neill in the Netherlands, I had the incredible opportunity to immerse myself in the world of sports and lifestyle branding. Continuing my pursuit of excellence, I embarked on an enriching Graphic Design Internship at Liefery in Berlin. Working with cross-functional teams, I contributed to various projects, including crafting compelling Adobe Premiere videos for the Business Intelligence team and producing visually engaging materials for Marketing and HR initiatives using Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. This experience broadened my creative horizons and fostered my ability to adapt my design style to diverse contexts.

Recently, I embarked on a new venture, completing the Google Data Analyst Course, which further ignited my passion for data-driven decision-making. Armed with practical knowledge in data analysis, data visualization, and Google Analytics, I am well-equipped to extract valuable insights and make data-informed decisions that drive meaningful outcomes. Furthermore, I have achieved the Microsoft PL-300 certification, solidifying my expertise in the Microsoft Power Platform and empowering me to leverage advanced tools for data analysis and business intelligence. As I continue to evolve in both design and data analytics, my mission is to create innovative solutions that seamlessly blend creativity and data insights. I am determined to contribute my unique skill set to make a positive impact and drive success in any project or team I am a part of. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I invite you to explore my work and accomplishments. If you have any inquiries, collaboration opportunities, or exciting projects on the horizon, I would be thrilled to hear from you. Let's connect and together, let's transform visions into reality!
